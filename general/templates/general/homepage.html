About Us

Diaspora suites and events garden is an exclusive lodging / events centre located in heart of Toga Village, Badagry. We take pride in the comfort of our clients and everything is being prepared to ensure their safety and comfort

Diaspora Suites provides the full complements of a private accommodation facility. We cherish the privacy of our guest. Our facility has a Stand-by generator plant and other facilities to keep our guests relaxed. Our suites are Code-named with Notable Cities/Precious Pearls, (BROOKLYN, WARWICK, COLUMBIA HEIGHTS, MANCHESTER, LEICESTER, MACEDONIA, EMIRATES and EMERALD, to impress a sense of identity, fantasy and lasting impression on the minds of our esteemed guests when entering the suites

Diaspora suites and events garden is an exclusive lodging / events centre located in heart of Toga Village, Badagry. We take pride in the comfort of our clients. Diaspora Suites provides the full complements of a private accommodation facility. We cherish the privacy of our guests.

Diaspora Events: Space is also provided for the hosting of events in Diaspora Events Garden. Weddings, Marriage anniversary and other joyous occasions-devoid of religious activities require a favorable atmosphere to share the joy… Diaspora provides this conducive atmosphere at a negotiable rate. This out-door facility is within the premises and prospective clients would be guided on how to access the facility.

Booking, Check in & CHECK-OUTS: Management appeals to guests checking out earlier than their scheduled departure to notify Diaspora Suites within the first few hours of their arrival. Diaspora Suites Check-in time is 18:00 HRS , Which means that rooms shall be provided from this time onward only. If a guest arrives before the check-in time, Diaspora Suites may provide a room-subject to availability. Check-out time is 12:00 HRS. Should a guest wish to retain the room beyond this time - and the room is available up to 16:00 HRS, a charge of half the applicable tariff may be applied. After 16:00 HRS, The full applicable tariff will be charged. Cancellations for individual reservations must be received in writing, 24 hours to the day of arrival or no later than 12:00 HRS of the date of reservation. Should the guest fail to arrive without prior notification to Diaspora Suites, the Reservations department will apply a ‘NO SHOW’ charge, equivalent to a ONE NIGHT room charge. All visitors to our facility are required to be identified by our guests before granted access to the facility. Please notify the Reception of any visitors you may wish to entertain.

As a matter of policy, Diaspora Suites is a NO-SMOKING facility.
